# Business Case


## Project Title: The Artisan Vault
Digital Marketplace for Local Artisans

### Summary:

This project proposes creating a digital marketplace that connect local artisans with consumers seeking handcrafted goods. This system will aim to streamline product discovery, improve seller visibility, and provide a secure and scalable online purchasing experience. The business case demonstrates why this product should be approved based on pure strategic value, financial return (ROI) and feasibility.

### Problem or Opportunity

Local artisans lack affordable centralized platforms to show and sell their products. There are existing large marketplaces that impose high fees, complexing onboard and heavy competition, making it difficult for small curators to stand out. The opportunity is to create a specialized marketplace that highlights craftsmanship, preserves local culture and enables sustainable micro entrepreneurship.

### Business Benefits
#### Tangible Benefits:

* New revenue streams via commissions and premium seller subscriptions.
* Reduced marketing costs for artisans due to centralized platform traffic
* Automated order processing replacing manual, error-prone workflows

#### Inangible Benefits:
* Increased recognition of brand for the organization sponsoring the platform
* Strengthened community relationships and cultural representation
* Improved market trust through rating system and verified sellers

### Alignments with Organizational Goals

The project align with long-term  objectives that will empower communities, e-commerce diversification, and expanding digital services. 

### Scope Overview

The business case covers the initial development of the online marketplace, including vendor management and product listings, safe checkouts and customer accounts. It will  exclude physical logistics, warehousing and external payment gateways development. (*third party APIs will be used*)

### Cost Estimates

Preliminary estimates indicates developments, posting and support will require moderate initial investment with ongoing operational costs related to cloud hosting, payment processing and Technical Support.

### Expected ROI

The Return on Investment is expected within `12 to 18 months` through transaction fees, seller subscription plans and optional advertisement placements. Growth projections will increase as more artisans will join the platform in product catalog will expand.

### Risks

* Competition from already existing major e-commerce marketplaces.
* Initial low user adoption rate.
* Security vulnerabilities naturally inherit in online payment systems.

### Feasibility Summary (TELOS)

* Technical: Feasible using widely available web technologies and cloud hosting
* Economic: Benefits outweigh costs within projected time frame.
* Legal: Must comply with e-commerce, privacy, and consumer protection laws
* Operational: Stakeholders are prepared to adopt and maintain systems.
* Deliverable within a realistic 12 to 16 week development cycle.

#### Recommendation

Based on the opportunity, financial outlook, and feasibility analysis, the project should proceed to the next stages of the SDLC
