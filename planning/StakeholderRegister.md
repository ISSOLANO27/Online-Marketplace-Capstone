# Stakeholder Register

## Purpose

## Stakeholder List:

### Primary Stakeholders

#### Artisan (Seller)  

- **Interest:** Wants an easy platform to upload items, track availability, and receive customer requests.  
  
- **Influence:** High (system success depends on artisan adoption)  
  
- **Expectations:** Simple UI, reliable notifications, minimal data entry.  
- 
#### Customers (Buyers)

- **Interest:** Want to browse handmade items and request purchases easily.  
- 
- **Influence:** Medium  
  
- **Expectations:** Clean browsing experience, accurate availability, fast responses.

### Secondary Stakeholders

**Project Sponsor**  

- **Interest:** Ensures the project delivers the value described in the Business Case.  
  
- **Influence:** High  
  
- **Expectations:** MVP delivered on time and aligned with goals.

**Systems Analyst / Developer (Student)**  

- **Interest:** Responsible for building, designing, and documenting the system.  
  
- **Influence:** Medium 
- 
- **Expectations:** Clear requirements, stable environment, realistic scope.

**Administrator**  
- **Interest:** Maintains system data, settings, and support functions.  
  
- **Influence:** Medium  

- **Expectations:** Easy access to data, straightforward admin tools.


### Tertiary Stakeholders


**Future Artisans (Expansion Phase)**  

- **Interest:** May use the platform later. 
 
- **Influence:** Low  

- **Expectations:** Scalable, easy onboarding.

**Local Community / Cultural Sector**  


- **Interest:** Indirect benefits from showcasing local craftwork.  

- **Influence:** Low  

- **Expectations:** Visibility for artisans and authenticity of items.

**Regulatory / Compliance Entities**  


- **Interest:** Customer privacy and data protection.  

- **Influence:** Medium  

- **Expectations:** Proper handling of contact data.
  
  <br>
  <br>

| Stakeholder        | Engagement Level | Notes |
|--------------------|------------------|-------|
| Artisan            | Active           | Frequent use; provides content |
| Customers          | Passive/Active   | Depends on browsing frequency |
| Sponsor            | Leading          | Directs major decisions |
| Analyst/Developer  | Active           | Executes all SDLC tasks |
| Administrator      | Supportive       | Manages data and access |
| Future Artisans    | Neutral          | Will matter in Phase 2 |
| Compliance Entities| Neutral/Supportive | Only relevant for privacy |


## Comunication Approach

- **Artisan:** Regular check-ins and usability feedback.  
  
- **Sponsor:** Progress updates at milestones.  
  
- **Administrator:** Documentation and brief training. 
   
- **Customers:** No direct communication (interaction through UI only).
